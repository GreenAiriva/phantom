What is Social Cost?

Greenhouse Gas Cost in Turkey

The social cost of greenhouse gases quantifies the economic and environmental damage caused by emissions. The EPA’s SC-GHG report offers detailed calculations. In Turkey, land transport emissions cost an estimated $14 billion annually. GreenAiriva provides innovative technologies to reduce these costs and support sustainable, economically viable climate solutions.

The Urban Air Crisis

GreenAiriva Technology Edge

Urban air pollution demands complex, multi-layered solutions. While moss filters, ion towers, and DAC each offer partial benefits, they fall short in scale or scope. GreenAiriva bridges these gaps with real-time analytics, multi-pollutant adsorption, and solar power—offering a scalable, sustainable, and intelligent path for urban air purification.

What Happened to Our Atmosphere!

Atmospheric Pollutant Concentrations

Greenhouse gas concentrations like CO₂, CH₄, and N₂O have reached critical levels, threatening climate balance. GreenAiriva offers innovative adsorption technologies using advanced materials to selectively capture these gases. Supported by real-time analytics, its system provides sustainable, urban-focused solutions to improve air quality and combat climate change effectively.
