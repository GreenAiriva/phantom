Who We Are?

Who We Are

GreenAiriva works to improve urban air quality, protect public health, and make concrete, measurable contributions to the fight against climate change. We set a new standard in sustainable urbanism with data-driven technologies.

Good Health & Well-Being

We create healthier communities in cities by removing an average of 360 kg of particulate matter (PM) per device each year.

Sustainable Cities & Communities

We model cities with our sensor network and lead more sustainable urban transformation through real-time air quality and data analytics.

Climate Action

Each of our devices directly captures a minimum of 5 and up to 30 tons of CO2e emissions annually, making a concrete contribution to climate action.

Vision

Our vision at GreenAiriva is to take concrete and measurable steps for healthier, more livable, and sustainable cities, leading the way in environmental health and the fight against climate change. With our technology that directly contributes to SDG-3, SDG-11, and SDG-13, we reduce air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions, protecting public health. Our dream is to pioneer the world’s first zero-carbon-footprint operations and production processes, setting a new global standard for sustainability.

Mission

Our mission at GreenAiriva is to improve urban air quality and protect public health by offering innovative, science-based solutions that effectively capture harmful particles and greenhouse gases.

We are committed to supporting sustainable urban transformation by integrating advanced technology, data, and community engagement. By prioritizing measurable environmental outcomes, we aim to empower cities and organizations to reach climate targets, enhance quality of life, and promote a culture of sustainability.

Through continuous innovation, transparency, and collaboration, we strive to set new standards in clean air technologies and become a trusted partner for sustainable progress worldwide.

Why GreenAiriva?

  • Innovative Technology: We go beyond traditional Direct Air Capture (DAC) methods, offering a low-energy, physical adsorption-based solution.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: We enable smart city integration with instant air quality analytics.
  • Economic & Eco-Friendly: We create economic value for our clients through carbon credit generation, sustainability reporting, and commercialization of captured pollutants.
  • Global Vision: Fully aligned with the EU Green Deal and the 2050 Net Zero Emissions goal, GreenAiriva is scalable from Turkey to the world.

Social and Environmental Contribution

GreenAiriva operates year-round with nearly all its energy supplied by solar power, achieving an almost zero carbon footprint. Its filtration technology causes no additional environmental harm, and all components are highly durable. In terms of cost-effectiveness and climate benefit, GreenAiriva outperforms current greenhouse gas reduction alternatives, delivering 2–4 times greater impact than converting a CNG bus to electric. This makes it a sustainable, economical, and eco-friendly solution.
